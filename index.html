Frequently Asked Questions

What is a shared account?
Multiple people use the same ChatGPT Plus account (same login credentials), which reduces the cost for each person while maintaining full access to all premium features.
How many devices can I use?
You can log in on two devices: 1 phone and 1 PC simultaneously.
What about privacy?
Use temporary chat mode for privacy. Your conversations and data remain private and are not shared with other users.
How often do passwords change?
Passwords change roughly once a month. For longer plans (3+ months), you'll receive new passwords automatically.
Do you provide personal accounts?
No, I only provide shared accounts. This keeps costs low while maintaining full ChatGPT Plus functionality.
What browsers work best?

How do I get access after payment?
